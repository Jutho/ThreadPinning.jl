ThreadPinning.callonthreads — Method

Calls the function fn on all Julia threads and returns the results obtained on each thread as a ordered Vector{Any}.

Example:

julia> Pinning.callonthreads(getcpuid)
8-element Vector{Any}:
  3
  4
  5
  8
  2
 10
  6
  9

julia> pinthreads(1:Threads.nthreads())

julia> Pinning.callonthreads(getcpuid)
8-element Vector{Any}:
 1
 2
 3
 4
 5
 6
 7
 8
